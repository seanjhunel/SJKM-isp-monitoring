Common Issues & Quick Fixes

🔴 No Internet Connection

Restart your router by unplugging it for 30 seconds. Check if the LOS light is blinking red on your ONU.

🟡 Slow Internet Speed

Try connecting via ethernet cable to rule out WiFi issues. If still slow, submit a ticket above.

🔵 WiFi Not Showing

Check if your router is powered on. Try accessing 192.168.1.1 to verify router settings.

⚡ Intermittent Connection

This could be a signal issue. Note the times it drops and include in your ticket.
